ABOUT US

Panaderia Arqui, is a bakery dedicated to the creation of high quality baked goods with an authentic touch. Every day we bake breads, cakes, cookies and other sweet treats. Our commitment to tradition and excellence drives us to use the finest ingredients and the best baking techniques. Many years ago, in a quiet neighborhood called "Panadoville," a group of friends with a shared love of baking decided to embark on an exciting adventure. Between laughter and dreams, they created a bakery to be called "PanArte." The beginning was not easy. These friends, Pablo, Ana, and Martín, were passionate about baking, but none of them had commercial experience. However, their enthusiasm and the quality of their products were undeniable. They started in a small shop with a modest oven and a limited selection of breads and pastries.

MISSION

Our mission is to delight our customers with baked goods of the highest quality, made with fresh and carefully selected ingredients. We strive to keep the tradition of artisan baking alive, offering authentic flavors that convey the love of good food and freshly baked bread.

VISION

Our vision is to be the cozy haven where every bite becomes a journey of flavors that transports our customers to special moments in their lives. We want to be recognized for being the bakery that awakens nostalgia and joy through excellence in craftsmanship and constant innovation. We want to be the heart of the community, a place where people come together to share the passion for freshly baked bread and sweets, creating memories that last forever.
